About me

Creative by nature.
Precise by practice.

I'm Berfin Unesi — a multidisciplinary digital media designer based in Ottawa. I bring together design, technology, and storytelling to build experiences that genuinely connect with people.

Who I am

A designer who thinks
in systems and feels in detail.

I'm currently pursuing a Diploma in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College, where I've been sharpening my skills across UX research, visual design, web development, motion graphics, and photography.

My work lives at the intersection of design, technology, and communication — where every visual decision supports clarity, usability, and impact. I don't just make things look good; I make them work well and feel right.

I approach every project with a systems mindset — asking not just how something looks, but how it functions, communicates, and performs at every touchpoint. Guided by design thinking, I combine creativity with a results-driven approach.

What I do

Three disciplines.
One cohesive practice.

I work across UX design, visual design, and web development — not as separate silos, but as a unified approach to building digital experiences end to end.

01

UX & Product Design

From research and personas to user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes — I design products that are intuitive, accessible, and built around real user needs. Figma is my primary tool, FigJam my thinking space.

02

Web Design & Development

I bridge the gap between design and code. I build responsive websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — ensuring what I design is fully realised in the final product, pixel for pixel.

03

Visual & Brand Design

Brand identity, typography systems, print and digital assets — I design with Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, bringing a strong visual sensibility and understanding of grid and hierarchy to every piece.

What drives me

Enthusiasm & creativity.
Precision & professionalism.

Design without passion is decoration. Passion without discipline is noise. I aim for both — every time.

Creativity

I design with genuine enthusiasm.

Every project excites me in a different way. Whether it's an unexpected typographic choice, a colour system that just clicks, or a UX flow that finally feels effortless — I bring real creative energy to the work, not just professional competence.

Enthusiasm

Curiosity is my most reliable design tool.

I'm genuinely excited by the problems I work on. That curiosity leads me to ask better questions, explore unexpected directions, and stay engaged long after the obvious answer has been found.

Professionalism

Craft matters. Process matters more.

Beautiful outcomes come from rigorous process — research, iteration, feedback, refinement. I hold myself to a high standard of craft while staying flexible enough to pivot when the work demands it.

Purpose

Design should always serve someone.

I design for people, not portfolios. Every decision I make — from layout to interaction — is made with a real human on the other end of it in mind. That responsibility is what keeps the work meaningful.

My journey

How I got here.

A path shaped by curiosity, craft, and a love of visual communication.

Foundation Graphic Design

Graphic Design & Branding

My design foundation began with graphic design — learning typography, layout, colour theory, and brand systems. I built a deep appreciation for visual hierarchy and the power of a well-constructed identity.

Expansion Web & Dev

Web Design & Development

I expanded into web design and front-end development, combining my visual instincts with technical skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I learned how to design for responsiveness, performance, and real-world constraints.

Depth UX Design

UX & Product Design

UX opened up a whole new dimension of the work. Learning to research, map user flows, build design systems in Figma, and test with real users transformed how I think about every design decision I make.

Now Algonquin · 2026

Interactive Media Design — Algonquin College

Currently completing my diploma in Interactive Media Design, synthesising everything I've learned across UX, visual design, development, motion graphics, and photography into a cohesive, multidisciplinary practice.
